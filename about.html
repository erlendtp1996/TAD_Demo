About Us

Who we are

The team of student employees who work at Technology & Design complete creative projects to promote the programs, services, and departments of JMU University Unions. We create posters for Off Campus Life, brochures for JMU Conference Services, graphics for Madison Union, take photos at UPB events, make videos for the Dux Center, and are willing to try anything else our clients can imagine.

If you have a passion, create something from it,

Cynthia Kim, Project Manager

Through this unique employment opportunity, our students get hands-on experience with project management, digital media production, and content strategy. They hone their communication skills by working with real-world clients in a supportive learning community. From the interview process to the alumni networking opportunities, Technology & Design prepares students to become outstanding contributors in their chosen fields. We practice innovation and iteration through rapid prototyping and agile methodology. After graduation, many of our our students go on to start their own businesses or contribute their talents to help startup companies succeed.

Our teams

Team Decription
Cinematography We strive to meet the needs and expectations of the organizations within University Unions through the use of engaging and professional video production for past, present, and future JMU Dukes.
Content Strategy Serving as part documentarian and part journalist, the content strategist strives to effectively share the stories, news and accomplishments of both University Unions and Technology and Design with the entire JMU community in a compelling and unique way.
Gallery Madison Union Art Galleries seek to provide a space for all students to display their passion for photography across all disciplines so that they can share their unique experiences with the JMU community.
Graphic Design As a team, our mission as TAD Graphic Designers is to challenge ourselves with our work, capture new audience markets, and produce a final product with a strong craft, photography, illustration, and conceptualization that ultimately melds together our diverse approaches to design. Through these means, we influence the JMU community by embracing the future of design and creating game-changing work that causes our target audience to engage with the goals of our clients.
Management The project managers inspire all the teams to achieve success by organizing projects, deadlines, and resources. A project manager’s greatest skill is communication: keeping team members up to date, filling in the missing details in project requests, and checking in with clients to make sure they are happy with the final result.
Photography As photographers for Technology and Design, we aim to capture JMU’s most memorable moments in the highest photographic quality imaginable. Through teamwork and cooperation, we will fulfill the marketing needs of University Unions as clients demand our professional services. Our proficiency and creativity with the photographic medium enables us to do great things.
Web Development The web development team aims to provide organizations within University Unions the tools for effectively communicating with the JMU community through online media in an accessible, convenient, and meaningful way.
Writing The writing team creates content for the University Unions, Madison Union, and Festival websites, runs the writers’ blog at jmuunions.wordpress.com, and debates the fate of the Oxford comma.
